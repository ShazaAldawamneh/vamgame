Welcome to Vam Game

In this game you will try to save the pink player from the vampires, by shooting, running or hiding, SEE instructions before starting the game. The number of vampires will increase after winning a level. YOU NEED to register first to be able to play!

Instruction:

Click the Left Arro to move to Left

Click the Right Arro to move to Right

Click the Down Arro to move to Down

Click the Top Arro to move to Top

Click the Q key to disappear or hide

Click the Space Key to shoot

You can stop the game by clicking cancel when the a tap pop up on the top of the window, after you kill an enemy

Your match details will stored in your profile!
